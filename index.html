About Me

My name is Anna Riley, I am 27 years old and for the past twelve years I have worked in restaurants.

In the last few years, I have felt the utmost desire to ditch the industry altogether, though it has a tricky way of sucking you back in time and time again.

So six months ago, I had this revelation that life didn't have to be about schmoozing customers into buying more booze, I could change fields entirely--with or without a college degree.

And along came a coding boot camp, an entry way into a field I have always been interested in, but never felt qualified for. A career that might lead to a much needed change of pace in my life.
